Background

I grew up in a small town where I took care of our family animals and I was the second oldest of 9 children. From a young age I had many responsibilities. I understood early on that you can't get anywhere in life without hard work, and I learned to enjoy a good days work. In highschool I was involved in sports, theater, choir, and my church group's youth programs.
I attended Brigham Young University and obtained my Bachelor's Degree in Plant and Wildlife Sciences in May 2017. Soon after graduating from BYU I spoke to my brother in law who works in tech, and I became intrigued with computer programming. Soon after finishing at BYU, I started a night class through the Applied Technologies program at SLCC and learned HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL. In July of 2018 I started studying full time at an accelerated tech training school in Lehi. I have become passionate about web development and get better each day.
